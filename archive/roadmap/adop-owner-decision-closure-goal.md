# ADOP Owner Decision Closure Goal

Status: Complete

## Goal

Resolve the remaining owner decisions for `ADOP` publication so the publication-execution wave can close and another operator can proceed to real repo export and go-live without policy ambiguity.

## Complete When

- license is chosen and recorded
- repository host/owner/name/visibility are chosen and recorded
- public contribution posture is chosen and recorded
- public security intake posture is chosen and recorded
- public verification / CI scope is chosen and recorded
- every choice is reflected in the ADOP publication packet

## Out Of Scope

- performing live repository creation
- changing ADOP generic shelf structure
- changing downstream project-local overlays

## Failure Conditions

- one decision remains implicit or split across multiple docs
- policy choices conflict with already prepared public docs
- a decision requires reopening ADOP genericization or pre-public structure work

## Closure Rule

When this wave closes, `ADOP publication execution` may advance from packet-prepared to fully closed.

## Closure

- owner decisions were chosen and recorded
- packet/docs were aligned to those choices
